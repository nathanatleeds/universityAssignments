What is DBPS Ltd?

DBPSLtd or Direct Building and Plumbing Supplies Ltd is a top of the range builders merchant based in Derby. It is less than a 15 minute walk from the city centre.

Direct Building and Plumbing Supplies Ltd was created in 1998 and was a new rebrand of the previous builders merchant. Within a few years DBPSLtd was one of the leading builders merchant in Derby and was competing against some of the large franchises in the city. DBPSLtd sells a range of different products from boillers to bricks and cement to fittings. We sell many different tools, building/plumbing supplies and a range of different fttings.
